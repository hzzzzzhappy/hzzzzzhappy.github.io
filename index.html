About Me

I am Hanzhe Liang. My research interests include 3D anomaly detection and spatial intelligence, and my doctoral research will focus on drug repurposing and repositioning.

From 2023 to 2026, I completed my undergraduate studies in Shenzhen and received a dual degree in Science from Shenzhen University and Management from Audencia Business School.

I conducted research at the Computer Vision Institute of Shenzhen University and later at the Digital Twin Institute of East Tech University.

I will join the Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI) as a Ph.D. student in 2026.
