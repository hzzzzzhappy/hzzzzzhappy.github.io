About Me

I am Hanzhe Liang, born in 2005 in Changsha, Hunan, China. My research interests include 3D anomaly detection and spatial intelligence, and my doctoral research will focus on drug repurposing and repositioning.

From 2023 to 2026, I completed my undergraduate studies in Shenzhen and earned a dual degree in Science from Shenzhen University and Management from Audencia Business School, graduating one year ahead of schedule.

I conducted research at the Computer Vision Institute of Shenzhen University and later at the Digital Twin Institute of East Tech University, focusing on 3D vision and spatial intelligence.

I will join the Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI) as a Ph.D. student in 2026.
