About Me

I am Hanzhe Liang, born in 2005 in Changsha, Hunan, China. My research interests include 3D anomaly detection and spatial intelligence, and my doctoral research will focus on drug repurposing and repositioning.

From September 2023 to June 2026, I completed my undergraduate studies in Shenzhen and earned a dual degree in Science from Shenzhen University and Management from Audencia Business School, graduating one year ahead of schedule.

During this period, I conducted research at the Computer Vision Institute of Shenzhen University under the supervision of Can Gao and Jinbao Wang. In 2025, I studied at the Digital Twin Institute of East Tech University, where I explored recent advances in VLA and spatial intelligence under the guidance of Xin Jin.

I will join the Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI) as a Ph.D. student in late 2026. I welcome collaborations in 3D vision, anomaly detection, spatial intelligence, and computational drug discovery.
