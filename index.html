About Me

I am Hanzhe Liang, born in 2005 in Changsha, China. My research interests include 3D anomaly detection, spatial intelligence, and computational drug discovery.

From 2023 to 2026, I completed my undergraduate studies in Shenzhen and received a dual degree in Science (Shenzhen University) and Management (Audencia Business School), graduating one year early.

I conducted research at the Computer Vision Institute of Shenzhen University and later at the Digital Twin Institute of East Tech University. My future doctoral research will focus on drug repurposing and its connection to spatial intelligence.
